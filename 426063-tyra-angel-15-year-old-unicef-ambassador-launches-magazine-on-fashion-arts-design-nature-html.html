Tyra Angel: 15-Year-Old Entrepreneur, UNICEF Ambassador Launches Magazine

 Posted on March 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  618 words  |  Enda Jantzen
  • Tyra Angel is 15 years old but already has an illustrious career in modelling, singing, and entrepreneurship
  • Apart from running a hair business, she is set to launch a magazine titled Gorgeous and Cool
  • On top of that, Tyra is an award-winning international model, UNICEF, as well as UNESCO Ambassador

At 15 years of age, Tyra Angel Odeny has already plunged into the world of publishing, something many teens her age can only dream of.

This is due to the fact that her magazine christened Gorgeous and Cool is set to be launched in the coming week at St Andrews School, Turi.

Multi-faceted

Hers is a venture that has not come by fluke, having done quite a lot for herself already.

The award-winning international model, UNICEF, and UNESCO Ambassador doubles up as the director of an organization that comprises the pageant, with the magazine being its latest venture.

Speaking to TUKO.co.ke's Hillary Lisimba, the young ambitious model, singer, and entrepreneur explained that the magazine revolves around fashion, arts and design, nature, culture, beauty and the modelling industry.

"I wanted to do a magazine because it unites the world with various wonders and unique talents," she said.

Tyra added that she looks forward to leveraging the magazine to bring the world together for the good.

Funds it through hair business

Her mother Jayne Okoth disclosed that her daughter funds her projects through proceeds from a hair business she runs with the brand name of Miss Tee.

She added that the young girl has been putting the magazine together for around seven months.

I decided to give individuals their own pages to express themselves and refined it as the editor. However, my biggest challenge has been timing when it comes to submitting stories as some featured individuals delayed," she recalled.

Tyra further expressed that she was inspired to do the publication to expose the different and unique talents that exist around the globe.

Illustrious modelling career

Tyra's journey into pageantry started at the tender age of four where she started modelling for fashion designers and children's shows.

By the age of 11 she could strut the catwalk like a professional model, could sing, and her love of fashion became true.

It is also during this period that Tyra was crowned as the Little Miss Kenya in 2017.

"At 12, she went to compete at the international pageants in Greece where 65 countries were represented. Tyra won the Grand Prix Little Miss United World 2018 with a Golden Crown," her mother remembered.

With the awards and shows coming in fast and furiously, her journey into responsibilities started, among them philanthropy where she helps the needy through charity projects and motivational talks.

When Tyra is not modelling or publishing she sings and has so far released two songs; Tabasamu, which won the Golden Lauren and Soy La Capitana (I am the Captain) which she performed in Spain in 2019.

Laura Tendo

In a separate story, 13-year-old Laura Tendo is a Class Seven pupil who's won several pageantries and used the crowns to run philanthropic projects around Vihiga county.

To keep children from disadvantaged backgrounds in school, she works with the help of a well-wisher known as Bethel Network to donate textbooks and sanitary pads to different schools.
