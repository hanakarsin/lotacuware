SportPesa unveils KSh 12 million Jackpot winner 2 months after resuming business

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  614 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

- Nairobi businessman Mutuku Muoka bagged KSh 12,444,453 in SportPesa's midweek Jackpot

- The spare parts dealer who is a Chelsea FC and Gor Mahia FC fanatic correctly predicted outcome of 13 games

- Those who scored 10 earned KSh 13,448, KSh 76,724 for 11 correct answers while those who missed the mark with just a point were paid KSh 456,694

Mutuku Muoka, a Nairobi based car spare parts dealer, has been announced winner of SportPesa's midweek Jackpot.

Mouka stormed into victory after he correctly predicted the outcome of 13 games giving him the golden opportunity to pocket KSh 12,444,453.

The 45-year-old businessman from Nairobi's Embakasi area said he has been a loyal customer with Kenya’s leading gaming platform and his fortune finally turned around, to get his year off to a perfect start.

He becomes the first Kenyan to win the midweek Jackpot about two months after SportPesa resumed its operations in Kenya.

As separately reported by TUKO.co.ke, the giant gaming platform alongside its close rival Betin closed shop in Kenya in mid-2019 following a tax dispute with Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA).

“When I got the call in the middle of the night from the SportPesa Customer Care, I could not believe what I was hearing. It was impossible to sleep after that,” Mouka stated.

The winner is a staunch football fan and locally he supports 18-time Kenya Premier League champions Gor Mahia FC but admits that he possesses a love for Tusker FC.

He also follows Kariobangi Sharks FC and he is hopeful that local football can slowly become as passionately followed as the likes of the English Premier League.

Internationally, he is a fan of Chelsea FC and admits that he is not one to just randomly place wagers on teams until he does enough analysis.

Devout gamer since 2016

Mouka admits that he first started playing in 2016 but became more hopeful and optimistic after he witnessed Samuel Abisai win SportPesa’s Mega Jackpot worth a whopping Ksh 221 million.

“Now that God has been good and allowed me to win this money. My dream is to expand my car parts business as I see what else I can invest in for the future,” he added.

Sportpesa midweek jackpot

The SportPesa midweek jackpot starts at KSh 10 million and increases each week until the winner is found.

It consists of 13 select games of the week which punters are required to correctly predict their outcome. The full amount on offer is won by the person who scoops all the correct prediction.

SportPesa also pays bonuses for good attempts. Bonus payouts start from 10 correct predictions. For last week's jackpot, those who got 10 correct predictions earned KSh 13,448, 11 correct predictions earned punters KSh76,724 while those who were off the mark by one match were paid KSh 456,694.

How to play

Gamers are required to use paybill number 5212121 or 955100 to recharge their wallets and start betting on SportPesa's website.

Punters can also bet via SMS shortcode 29050.
