The Rock, Scarlett Johansson top Forbes list of highest paid actors, actresses

 Posted on February 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  512 words  |  Enda Jantzen

- Renowned business magazine, Forbes, has released a list of the world’s highest paid actors and actresses in 2020

- Interestingly, actor Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson and actress Scarlett Johnson topped the list

- The 47-year-old Johnson earned a whopping KSh 8.9 billion while 35-year-old Johansson earned KSh 5.6 billion

There is no doubt that acting, especially in Hollywood, comes as one of the top career paths for talented actors, some of whom are now ranked among the world’s wealthiest people.

This has been confirmed by Forbes which recently featured names of the top paid actors and actresses in the world as at February 2020.

The actors' list was topped by 47-year-old former WWE champion, Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson, who earned a staggering KSh 8.9 billion before tax deductions.

In number two was Chris Hemsworth who earned KSh 7.6 billion, followed by Robert Downer Junior who made KSh 6.6 billion to close the top three standings.

In the female category, the list was topped by 35-year-old Avengers star Scarlett Johansson, who earned KSh 5.6 billion according to Forbes.
