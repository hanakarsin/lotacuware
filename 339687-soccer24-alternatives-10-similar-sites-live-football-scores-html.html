Soccer24 alternatives: 10 similar sites for live football scores

 Posted on March 29, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1691 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Soccer24 is an online service that provides live soccer scores, results, fixtures, and other football information from leagues across the globe. You can get data from leagues in Asia, Africa, Europe, Australia, North, and South America. The site covers major leagues and lower divisions of some of the most popular football countries. But Soccer24 has other competitors too.

Sites similar to Soccer24 also offer live football scores from all over the world. You can use them to get information about goal scorers, red cards, halftime results, and goal alerts. That makes it easier for you to follow a football match live without necessarily having to watch it on TV. If you are a punter, you can use the same services to follow your bets and get immediate information on changes like substitutions and player injuries, among others.

SofaScore is a live sports service that gives you live coverage of ongoing sports events.
