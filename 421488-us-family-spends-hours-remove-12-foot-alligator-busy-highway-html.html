A family in Louisiana has shared they were shocked to find a 12-foot alligator on a busy highway and had to ask help from Good Samaritans to wheedle it off the busy road.

Family bumps into a huge alligator

The incident happened on July 28, and a family coming from a vacation spotted the huge animal on the road and almost hit it, thinking it was a huge tyre left on the road.

Amanda Carter, who works with an animal rescue, was travelling back to Livingston Parish from Texas, and she posted the video of the alligator on her TikTok page.

In the shared video clip, Carter and her family, together with other strangers, could be seen trying to wheedle the huge animal safely out of the busy highway.

According to Carter, they called for help from security officials, and as they were waiting for them to arrive, they tried to remove the alligator using a stick while also feeding it with Turkey sandwiches.

Carter also shared her experience with WBRZ TV, and she narrated what she felt upon seeing the alligator.

"It was pretty remarkable. My kids are still telling jokes, like, why did the alligator cross the road?" Carter told WBRZ.
