Marty Meierotto biography

Marty was born on 10th July 1966 in northern Wisconsin, USA. His parents are Thomas T Meierotto (father) and Karen Meierotto (mother). He has two siblings, Jeff Meierotto and Steve Meierotto. However, he has not offered any information about their current whereabouts.

After completing his elementary and high school education, Marty did not get the opportunity to join college. He gave up on his education to maintain their family name in the business.

Career

Marty gained interest in his family business from a young age. After completing his high school education, he ventured into the industry as a full-time trapper. Unfortunately, it was difficult to perform his work in North Wisconsin because there were few animals to catch, making the competition high.

As a result, he decided to search for a place with an abundance of animals. Together with his brother, Jeff, Marty gathered all their savings to relocate to Alaska. In 1985, the duo drove away and settled in the cold state of Alaska.

Life in Alaska

Initially, their idea was to move to Alaska to try trapping for a short period. However, as time went by, he continued falling in love with the land. Marty started doing several odd jobs to earn more money that will in return help in his trapping venture.

He started working as a janitor in Fairbanks. He also took construction and logging jobs to make more money for food and buy his first trap and tent. This would enable him to go trapping during his free time. After a short while, he built himself a small cabin that was easier to warm in cold Alaska.

He worked for a couple of years before quitting his job to focus on becoming a full-time trapper. Marty focused on setting trap lines and catching animals for fur. Most of these animals were based in an area 200 miles north of Fairbanks whose temperatures were 30-40 degrees below zero.

Other endeavours

Even though he is a full-time trapper, the trapping season is not always in full swing, especially during summer. During this period, he works with the Alaska Fire Service as a smokejumper. This entails travelling to various locations in the state to fight fires.

He has earned the title of a smokejumper since he often uses parachutes to access different fire areas. He enjoys the job because it is not challenging for him as he is familiar with most of Alaska. In addition, the job comes in handy in providing extra income to support his wife and daughter.

Marty Meierotto's plane crash

Marty Meierotto cabin is located close to the Draanjike river on the Northern slope of Alaska. He visits the house every month using his Piper PA-18A-150 super club aircraft with well-equipped tundra tires.
