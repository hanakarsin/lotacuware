Popular Nairobi diaries actress, Pendo, throws Vera Sidika under the bus

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  314 words  |  Hettie Henneman

- Vera Sidika lied about the reasons why she left reality TV show, Nairobi diaries 

- According to Pendo, a fellow cast member, Vera was blaming her shortcomings on cast members instead of just leaving 

- The reality show does not work with a script like Vera had earlier intimated 

On Tuesday, February 24 2016, Vera Sidika parted ways with Nairobi diaries. She announced on her social media that she would no longer  be part of  the cast and would not be on the second season of the Nairobi diaries.

In her updates, Vera accused  the producers of the show of using her. The socialite wrote that she was tired of being ordered around and following a script that was not in line with what she wanted.

READ ALSO: Why 21-year-old Vera Sidika has quit Nairobi diaries

Her fans online were devastated after her announcement.

In defense of Nairobi diaries, Pendo has revealed that Vera Sidika was lying  of the reasons why she quit the show. She went on to revel that:

1. Nairobi diaries does not run on a script.

2. No one is forced to do anything when they are on set.

3. Vera was quitting under  her own volition but chose to blame her exit on the cast.

Pendo is happy and excited with the new developments. She says that the rest of the cast will continue to shoot as planned.

Pendo has been on  a feud with Vera Sidika in the past. In January 2016, Vera Sidika and her personal body guard suffered injuries after Pendo attacked them with a beer bottle.

On a related incident, Vera Sidika reported that the cast was jealous of her. She went on to say that she was the only reason that Kenyans watched the show.
