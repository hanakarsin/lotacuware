I Been Like You But This One Pain Me

 Posted on June 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  300 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Popular Nollywood actor, Chidi Mokeme has revealed what the presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar did that drove him to tears.

The Tv host stated in a Twitter post on Tuesday that Atiku made an insensitive move when he deleted a post condemning the murder of Deborah Samuel.

Naija News recalls that on May 12, 2022, Deborah Samuel a second-year Christian student was lynched and set ablaze by her colleagues at Shehu Shagari College of Education, Sokoto, over alleged blasphemy.

After her death, a post from Atiku Abubakar‘s official accounts condemned the lynching.

However, after some of his followers threatened not to vote for him because he condemned the lynching, Atiku deleted the post and said he did not approve of it being posted.

Reminiscing on the incident, Mokeme while in tears said that he was hurt after Atiku decided to delete the tweet.

He stated that he was once a supporter of Atiku but that incident made him stop supporting the PDP flag bearer.

Rico Swavey In Critical Condition After Ghastly Accident

Former Big Brother Naija Housemate, Rico Swavey, is currently battling for life after being involved in a ghastly accident.

Naija News reports that this was made known on Tuesday in a post shared on Twitter by his colleague, Alex Unusual as she calls for prayers.

According to Alex, the reality TV star was involved in an accident a few hours ago and the doctors are still experiencing difficulty in trying to resuscitate him at the hospital.

She tweeted: “Good morning sweethearts. Please put @iamricoswavey in your prayers today. He was involved in a ghastly accident hours ago and the doctors are still trying to resuscitate him as I just left the hospital. It’s really critical. I’m staying positive.#PrayForRicoSwavey”
