Samsung Galaxy M20 hardware

Check out Samsung Galaxy M20 all details of the devices hardware components.

Design

This phone presents the trademark design of Samsung phones. It has a plastic frame with front glass, and the volume and power buttons are located on the right side where they are easily reachable. Samsung Galaxy M20 colors are limited to two; Ocean Blue and Charcoal Black. Compared to other phones in the mid-range division, it is relatively heavier and weighs 186g. One can easily mistake it to be in the category of the high-end smartphones, and that gives it an edge over its key competitors such as Redmi Note 7.

Display

The device has a display unit size of 6.3 inches equivalent to an area of 97.4 square cm. The phone has PLS TFT capacitive touchscreen with 16M colors which enables you to view images of a wide range of colors. Its screen resolution is 1080 x 2340 pixels with an aspect ratio of 19.5:9 ratio and a pixel density of 409 ppi.

Processor

It has the Exynos 7904 (14nm) chipset and Mali-G71 MP2 GPU. The central processing unit is Octa-core: 2x1.8 GHz Cortex-A73 & 6x1.6 GHz Cortex-A53). This means that the phone has a considerably high processing speed which makes it suitable for gaming.

Storage

The memory features of the Samsung Galaxy M20 are quite remarkable and ideal for people with high storage needs. The phone has a memory card slot which can allow up to 1 TB microSD. Further, it has a ROM of 32 GB and 3 GB RAM, or 64 GB and 4 GB RAM.

Battery

Not so many Samsung smartphones come with high battery capacity, but the Samsung Galaxy M20 does. The device has a non-removable Li-Po 5000mAh battery, and it has an endurance rating of 103h, which is pretty good compared to similar phones in the mid-range category. Additionally, it has a USB Type-C port which supports a quick charge of approximately 2 hours from 0 to 100%. Samsung Galaxy M20 battery life and fast charge features make it impressive and suitable for people used to intensive use of phones.

Speaker & call quality

Samsung Galaxy M20 speaker provides high-quality audio which enables you to get the best call quality. The loudspeaker is properly functional and audible enough. Alternatively, you can connect the headphones to the 3.5 mm jack and listen to audio.

Samsung Galaxy M20 software

What software features does the device have?

OS and features

Samsung Galaxy M20 Android version is 8.1 (Oreo). Even though it is an outdated version, it is upgradable to Android 9.0 (Pie); One UI. The remarkable connectivity features of the phone include Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and GPS. You will also appreciate how various sensors such as fingerprint, gyro, accelerometer, compass and proximity work perfectly.

Camera, video and image quality

It might not have one of the best cameras in the Samsung M series, but the camera is good enough to capture relatively high-quality images and videos. The main camera has a 13 MP primary sensor and a 5 MP ultra-wide sensor while the selfie camera is 8 MP. It has a front and back LED flash to provide light when capturing images.
