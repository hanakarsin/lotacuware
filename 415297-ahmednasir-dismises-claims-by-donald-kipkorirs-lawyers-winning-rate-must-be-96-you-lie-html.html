Ahmednasir Dismisses Claims by Donald Kipkorir's that Lawyer's Winning Rate Must Be 96%: &quot;You Lie&quot;

  • Ahmednasir Abdullahi, however, said Kipkorir's argument would only make sense in a developed country
  • According to Abdullahi, cases in poor developing countries are not won based on competence

A legal professional who wins one out of 20 cases deserve to be fired, city lawyer Donald Kipkorir has said.

The flamboyant advocate argued that any lawyer whose winning rate is below 96 % is either incompetent or put up an incompetent defence.

"An excellent and honest lawyer will always advise his client of the viability of the claim/defence. If your lawyer loses 1/20 Cases, fire him. A lawyer’s track record should be 96%," he claimed.

However, according to senior counsel Ahmednasir Abdullahi, Kipkorir's argument would only make sense in a developed country where cases are won based on competence.

"In the developed world like America, UK, EU...but in poor counties like Kenya, the judge is the determinant factor....I tell the truth. You lie easily as you inhale oxygen...that is the difference...don't forget we lawyers know one another...the cases done, those won, those lost etc...ati a lawyer winning rate must be 96%," he responded.

Elsewhere, Ahmednasir recently claimed the Supreme Court of Kenya was the Judiciary's weakest link in the dispensation of justice.

In particular, it would take a miracle for the Court of Appeal to overturn the judgment on the Building Bridges Initiative (BBI) process.
