Hanna Cavinder - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on April 3, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  152 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Social star who is best known for the CavinderTwins TikTok account that she shares with her twin sister Haley. She and her sister post dance routines as well as comedy skits that often revolve around twin situations. Their account has amassed over 4 million fans. She and Haley are also longtime basketball teammates and they often adorn basketball jerseys in their posts. 

Before Fame

She and her sister launched their YouTube channel in January 2020 and their first video was a Q&A. 

Trivia

She began playing college basketball at Fresno State University in 2019. She was a 2020 All-Freshman Team selection and she earned All-Mountain West honors in both 2020 and 2021. 

Family Life

Her three other sisters, Brooke, Brandi, and Natalie, have all appeared on her TikTok account, as have her father, Tom, and her mother, Katie. 

Associated With

She posted a dancing TikTok video set to the Pop Smoke song "Hello" in August 2020.
