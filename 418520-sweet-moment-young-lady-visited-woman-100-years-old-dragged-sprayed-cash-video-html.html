Priest builds house for 90-year-old woman after she gave him six eggs

A 90-year-old widow, Mama Agatha Awua, was gifted a house by a parish priest of St Augustine, Demekpe in Markurdi, Reverend Fr Solomon Mfa Ukeyima.

Solomon explained that Mama Agatha paid him a condolence visit with a gift of six native eggs when armed robbers invaded the father's house even though that was not her parish.

Consequently, he said that motivated him to make a promise to God that he was going to build a place for the woman before she passed on.
