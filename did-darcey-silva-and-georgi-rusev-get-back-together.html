Darcey and Georgi Can't Seem To Stay Away From Each Other for Good on 'Darcey &amp; Stacey'

Did Darcey and Georgi get back together on 'Darcey & Stacey'? There was a secret hookup and now, fans want to know what their relationship status is.

The more things change on Darcey & Stacey, the more they stay the same. At least, when it comes to Darcey Silva and her twin sister Stacey Silva's drama. And, of course, when it comes to Darcey's relationship woes with ex, Georgi Rusev. But in a recent episode, Georgi reveals they had a secret hookup in Miami and now, we have to know ‚Äî did Darcey and Georgi get back together?

Up until now, Darcey has been adamant about saying goodbye to her past for good. But when Stacey's husband, Florian Sukaj, invites Georgi to their wedding festivities, it means that Darcey and Georgi are forced to be around each other and face their past together. It also means that there might still be something there between them. This is like a really bad version of The Parent Trap.

Did Darcey Silva and Georgi Rusev get back together?

In the April 3 episode of Darcey & Stacey, after Georgi and Darcey come face-to-face during Stacey and Florian's joint bachelor / bachelorette party, Georgi admits to friends that he and Darcey hooked up a little while back. According to him, he and Darcey met up one night while he was in Miami and they slept together. He had hoped it meant they were on the road to getting back together.

However, Darcey tells him in the same episode, it was supposed to be a one night stand. She says she has moved on and she doesn't want to be with him. But Georgi believes there's hope and he doesn't seem intent on giving up that ounce of hope any time soon. It doesn't look like Darcey and Georgi are together outside of Darcey & Stacey. But they could be keeping it all on the down-low until a potential fifth season.

Darcey tries dating other men in Season 4 of 'Darcey & Stacey.'

Season 4 is the first time Darcey has really dated casually. Before Georgi, Darcey had a couple of other boyfriends who were less than ideal partners for her. And both of those toxic relationships played out on TV, which made them even more difficult for her to handle. Especially when they crashed and burned.

But in Season 4 of Darcey & Stacey, Darcey dates other men and plays the field in a way viewers hadn't seen before. There's even a guy she talks about during Stacey and Florian's party that piques the interest of Georgi, who believes he and Darcey might get back together soon.

Georgi is in Stacey's wedding with Darcey.

Even though Darcey spends the majority of Season 4 being happily single and embracing the world of dating, things get complicated when Florian reveals that her ex is part of the wedding. But, while Georgi's return could have been much more detrimental for Darcey, in the end, she's able to have a civil talk with him about continuing to go their separate ways.

Unfortunately, Georgi isn't quite on the same wavelength. And viewers may have not seen the last of Darcey's ex.
