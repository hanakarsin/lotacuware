Man builds huge family compound of over 100 rooms and hospital, gifts them to his kinsmen

 Posted on February 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  466 words  |  Hettie Henneman

- A generous man, Ahmed Raji, has built a cluster of houses in the same compound for his kinsmen

- The compound which consists of 100 rooms also has modern-day facilities like a community health centre

- Raji began the renovation during the middle of 2020 and completed it all within a year

A Nigerian man, Ahmed Raji, has put a big smile on his kinsmen’s faces as he built a big family compound with 100 rooms.

The philanthropist did this as a way to close off the year 2020 as he handed over the keys of the buildings situated at Oke-Esa, Iseyin, Oke Ogun in Oyo state to his kinsmen.

According to Adeolu Akande, who commissioned the project and shared photos on his Facebook page, the man earlier, in 2020, demolished the old buildings in the compound.

The total transformation came with a borehole as residents of the compound now have a good water source.

To show that he is very much concerned about their health, he also built a hospital for them.
