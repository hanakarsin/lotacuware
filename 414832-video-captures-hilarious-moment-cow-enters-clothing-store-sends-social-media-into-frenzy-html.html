- A trending video has captured the awkward moment a cow stormed a clothing store and disrupted its activities

- The animal seemed to have gone shopping as it went for some clothing materials and littered the floor before running off

- The funny clip generated massive reactions from social media users with some wondering how the cow gained access to the store

It is the joy of every shop owner to receive huge number of customers each day, but for a certain shop owner in Nigeria, that wasn't the case.

This was after a cow showed up at an unidentified store, leaving the store owner confused.

In a video that has gone viral on social media, the cow seemed to have gained access from a different entry point as it didn't appear through the front door.
